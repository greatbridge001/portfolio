Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before hiring me. Still have questions? Just reach out!

How long does it take to build my website?

It depends on the complexity of the project. A basic 3-page website typically takes 3–5 days. A standard site with blog and forms takes 1–2 weeks. Custom web applications are scoped individually. I always give you a clear timeline upfront before we begin.

Do you work with clients outside Nairobi or Kenya?

Absolutely! I work with clients both locally in Nairobi and remotely across Kenya and internationally. All communication, project updates, and deliveries happen smoothly online via WhatsApp, email, and video calls.

What information do you need to start my project?

I'll need a brief description of your business, the goals for your website, any branding materials you have (logo, colors), content like text and images, and examples of websites you like. I guide you through the process step-by-step if you're unsure.

Can I update the website content myself after launch?

Yes! For sites that require regular updates, I build in a CMS (Content Management System) so you can easily add pages, update text, upload images, and manage blog posts yourself — no coding knowledge needed.

Are your prices negotiable?

Yes! My listed prices are starting points. Every project is unique, and I'm happy to discuss a custom quote that fits your specific needs and budget. Reach out and let's talk — I'm flexible and always aim for a fair deal for both of us.

Do you provide hosting and domain name services?

I can assist you with setting up hosting and purchasing a domain name, and I'll handle the full deployment and setup of your website on your hosting server. I recommend trusted, reliable hosting providers that fit your budget.
